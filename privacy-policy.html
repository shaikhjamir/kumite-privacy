Kumite Punch Tracker — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

Kumite Punch Tracker is built to keep your data on your devices. We do not operate servers, and we do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

Data stored on your devices

Training sessions, punch metrics, heart-rate samples, fighter profiles, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch and synced directly between your own devices using Apple's WatchConnectivity. This data never reaches us or any third party.

Health and motion data

With your permission, the Watch app reads motion data to detect punches and heart-rate data during workouts via Apple HealthKit. Workouts you record can be saved to Apple Health on your device. Health data is used only to provide app functionality, never for marketing or advertising, and is never transmitted off your devices by us.

Optional sign-in

You may optionally sign in with Apple ID. The resulting name and email address (if you choose to share them) are stored only on your device to personalize the app and are not transmitted to us.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through your App Store account. We do not receive your payment details. Apple's handling of purchase data is described in Apple's privacy policy.

Analytics and tracking

The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking.

Data deletion

You can delete sessions and profiles in the app at any time, and deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Changes

If our practices change (for example, if we add online features), this policy will be updated before those features ship.
